EggRice

Egg rice is a simple, quick, and healthy recipe.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Green chilli
  • Onions
  • Garam Masala
  • Ginger Paste
  • Cup of Boiled Rice
  • Lemon

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Then add green chilies & onion. Saute for 2 minutes on medium flame.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and mix well, fry until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Now add in turmeric and salt. Mix well.
  5. Keep on medium flame. Add the eggs and mix slowly.
  6. Now add garam masala and give a nice mix until the egg becomes like a scrambled egg texture.
  7. Add the prepared boiled rice, coriander leaves & lemon juice.
